What is a BMI Calculator?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a measure of body fat based on your weight and height. It helps assess whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. The formula to calculate BMI is weight in kilograms divided by the square of height in meters. Use the calculator above to find your BMI and understand your body's composition better.
